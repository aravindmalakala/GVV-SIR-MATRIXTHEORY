\textbf{Question}:\\
Find the direction and normal vectors of the following line:\\
x+2y=6
\\
\solution \\
\begin{table}[h!]

For the line of form Ax+By+C=0 the normal vector is given by
\begin{align}
    n&=\myvec{A\\B}\\
    A=1,B=2\\
    \implies n&=\myvec{1\\2}
\end{align}
Let the direction vector be d=$\myvec{a\\b}$
\begin{align}
    d^T.n&=0\\
    a+2b&=0\\
    \implies d&=\myvec{-2\\1}
\end{align}
