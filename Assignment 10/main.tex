\textbf{Question}:\\
Find the area of the region bounded by $y^2=2x$ and $x^2+y^2=4x$.
\\
\solution
\begin{table}[h!]

The parameters of the conics are
\begin{align}
V_1=\myvec{0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1}\;,\;u_1=\myvec{-\frac{1}{2}\\0} \;,\;f_1=0\\
V_2=\myvec{1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1}\;,\;u_2=\myvec{2\\0}\;,\;f_2=-4
\end{align}
The intersection of two conics with parameters $V_i,u_i,f_i,\;i= 1,2$ is defined as
\begin{align}
x^T\brak{V_1+\mu V_2}x+2\brak{u_1+\mu u_2}^T x + \brak{f_1+\mu f_2}\;=\;0
\end{align}
Solving this the points of intersection are 
\begin{align}
\myvec{0 \\ 0}\;,\;\myvec{2 \\-2}\;,\;\myvec{2 \\ 2}
\end{align}
Area between the curves is,
\begin{align}
2\int_{0}^{2} \brak{ 2 + \sqrt{4-y^2}-\frac{y^2}{2}} \, dy 
\end{align}
By solving the integration, we get area is equal to 11.62 sq.units
